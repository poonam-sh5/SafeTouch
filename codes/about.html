About SafeTouch

SafeTouch is a platform dedicated to raising awareness about safe touch and ensuring the protection of children from abuse. Our mission is to provide a safe space for education, support, and resources related to good touch and bad touch.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child feels safe, heard, and empowered. Our goal is to educate children, parents, and caregivers about safe and appropriate boundaries to prevent abuse and foster healthy relationships.

Our Mission

Our mission is to:

  • Educate children on the importance of good touch vs. bad touch.
  • Provide resources for parents and caregivers to help protect children from abuse.
  • Offer a platform for open dialogue on sensitive topics of touch and boundaries.

Our Values

We believe in:

  • Empowerment: Enabling children and adults to make informed decisions about their bodies.
  • Awareness: Spreading knowledge to prevent harm and create safe environments.
  • Support: Offering a non-judgmental space where individuals can find help and understanding.
